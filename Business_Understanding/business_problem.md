Business Context:
The dataset represents a global retail company operating across multiple regions and selling products under different categories such as Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology. The company wants to analyze sales performance to improve revenue and profitability.
Problem Statement:
The objective of this analysis is to evaluate regional and category-wise sales performance using real retail data to identify underperforming areas, growth opportunities, and actionable strategies to support business decision-making.
Business Objectives:
• Analyze sales and profit across different regions  
• Identify top and low-performing product categories  
• Understand sales trends over time  
• Detect regions with high sales but low profit margins  
• Recommend data-driven business actions
KPIs:
• Total Sales  
• Total Profit  
• Profit Margin (%)  
• Sales Trend (Monthly)  
• Category Contribution (%)
